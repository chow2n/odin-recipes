Rice

Description

How to make delicious and scrumptious white rice in a state-of-the-art rice cooker.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Rice Cooker
  • Water

Steps

  1. Make sure rice cooker bowl and top are clean.
  2. Fill rice cooker with desired cups of rice.
  3. Rinse uncooked rice with water 3-4 times or until water looks clear.
  4. Fill rice cooker with water to designated fill line relative to the cups of rice you are cooking.
  5. Press cook and in 10-20 minutes you will have rice.
