Chicken Stir Fry

Description

This is a delicious dish that can be made with chicken thighs or chicken breast and your favorite vegetables. It is a quick and easy recipe and perfect for those busy weekdays.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast/Thigh
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Shallots
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Corn Starch/Potato Starch
  • Broccoli

Steps

  1. Start by preparing all of the ingredients. Mince the garlic and ginger, cube and dry the chicken, slice the shallots, clean and chop the broccoli, and create a starch slurry with some water and starch.
  2. Next, heat up your pan and oil and throw the garlic in once the oil is warm.
  3. Then, after your garlic is fragrent, throw the chicken in and season with salt and pepper to taste. Add some garlic and onion powder and some paprika if you have it.
  4. After the chicken has cooked a little and is white, add in the garlic and broccoli.
  5. Cook everything in the pot for about 7-10 minutes and then add some soy sauce and a splash of sesame oil.
  6. Once everything is cooked, turn down the heat add in your starch slurry to thicken up the sauce.
