Twice Baked Potato

Description

These twice baked potatoes work perfectly as a simple, crowd-pleasing entree or a delicious, rich side dish.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Bacon
  • Sour Cream
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Green Onions

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F (175C).
  2. Bake potatoes until tender (about 1 hour).
  3. While the potatoes are baking, cook bacon on a skillet until evenly browned. Drain, crumble, and set aside.
  4. Once potatoes are finished, slice in half, and scoop meat into bowl. Save the skins for later.
  5. Add 1 cup sour cream, 1/2 cup milk, 4 tablespoons butter, 1/2 tablespoon salt and pepper, 1/2 cup cheese, and 1/2 of the green onions to the bowl of potatoes. Mix with a mixer until well blended and creamy.
  6. Spoon the mixture into the potato skins and top with cheese, bacon, and more green onions.
  7. Return potatoes to preheated oven and bake until cheese is melted (about 15 minutes).
